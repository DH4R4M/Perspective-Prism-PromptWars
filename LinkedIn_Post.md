🚀 Excited to share my submission for the Google #PromptWars Challenge! 

Introducing **Stadium Sentry** 🏟️ — a smart Event Management Dashboard designed to tame the chaos of massive live venues. 

When things go wrong at a stadium, unstructured, frantic reports flood the control room. Ground staff need actionable intelligence, instantly. To solve this, I leveraged the **Google Gemini API** (`gemini-2.5-flash`) to create a "Prism Logic Framework". 

Instead of generating a generic AI summary, Stadium Sentry ingests raw incident reports and simultaneously outputs customized perspectives for three critical stakeholders:
👮 **Security:** Highlighting crowd safety & deployment needs.
🎉 **Fan Experience:** Managing brand impact and visitor comfort.
👔 **Operations Manager:** Flagging logistical bottlenecks & liability.

To take it a step further, the AI actively evaluates the `Severity` of the report to dynamically alter the UI! A life-threatening emergency instantly triggers heavy red-alert pulses across our custom Vanilla CSS glass UI, while minor info drifts calmly.
